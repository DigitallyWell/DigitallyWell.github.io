SASTRO

Becoming Self-Aware and Strong

Mindfulness-Based Strengths Practice (MBSP) is an 8-week, evidence-based program that combines mindfulness and character strengths to help people flourish. It's the first to use mindfulness not only to manage stress, but to cultivate what's best in us - our signature strengths.

SASTRO is an adaptation of MBSP, especially for young people - teens and pre-teens. It provides bite-sized learning contents and a toolkit with an armour of tools to practice mindfulness and character strengths in our daily lives.

Though we know mindfulness and character strengths practice improves our wellbeing, it is challenging to make it part of our daily life, especially for young people. SASTRO is an evolving and experimental approach to embed these practices in our daily life.
